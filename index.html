Drop-Off Service

Laundry done in 3 easy steps

Step 1
Drop-Off Your Laundry

In store drop-off is available 7 days a week. We will weigh your clothes and charge you by weight (10 lbs minimum). Have special instructions? Just let us know!

Step 2
We Wash, Dry, and Fold

We take pride in all our processes to ensure a quality and consistent service each and every time. Our goal is simplicity and time savings for our customers.

Step 3
Pickup in Two Hours

We give you back your laundry crisp, clean, and ready to wear straight out of the bag. Take a load off, let us transform your laundry time into free time!
